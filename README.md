## project overview

This project is about creating social media pages on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok for a marketing agency team. The main goal is to market ourselves as a professional and creative marketing team while providing valuable educational content about marketing in an engaging and entertaining way. The project solves the problem of misinformation and lack of marketing awareness, helping business owners understand marketing correctly so they are not misled by unqualified marketers. It also supports startups and small businesses by guiding them on how to reach their target audience effectively and choose the right strategies. The project will be presented through active social media pages featuring educational posts, short videos, reels, and interactive content to attract and engage the audience.
